About.

Reward Charity is a simple and effective method for donating to the charities
which will maximize your donationand positively impact your community.

  • Community Empowerment Fund

    The Community Empowerment Fund (CEF) is a student-run non-profit based in Chapel Hill, NC. It provides savings opportunities, financial education, and support to unemployed and underemployed individuals.

  • Girl Develop It

    Help improve the technology industry's diversity problem by supporting women in technology. Girl Develop It (GDI) is the leading nonprofit organization providing accessible learning programs for adult women.

  • Paws4Ever

    Paws4ever is a guaranteed-adoption animal shelter and sanctuary located in Hillsborough, NC, dedicated to improving the lives of cats and dogs. They also provide Legacy Care, a program that cares for animals when owners are no longer able to.
